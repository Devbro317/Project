About Us

The South Eastern University of Sri Lanka's Career Guidance Unit is dedicated to assisting students in realizing their full potential and preparing them for successful careers. Our mission is to provide comprehensive career services, guidance, and resources that support the career development needs of our students and graduates.

Vision

Be a center of excellence in producing employable graduates by providing support, guidance and counseling

Mission

To facilitate the career development through professionalism, mannerism, unity and leadership and enabling them to meet employers expectation by providing variety of services for enhancing competencies needed by the world of work

Our Values

We believe in integrity, excellence, inclusivity, and collaboration. Our services are designed to uphold these values and ensure that all students have access to the resources and support they need to succeed.

What We Offer

We offer a wide range of services, including career counseling, resume and cover letter reviews, interview preparation, job search strategies, and networking opportunities. Our team is here to assist students at every step of their career journey.
