About Us

The South Eastern University of Sri Lanka's Career Guidance Unit is dedicated to assisting students in realizing their full potential and preparing them for successful careers. Our mission is to provide comprehensive career services, guidance, and resources that support the career development needs of our students and graduates.

Vision

Be a center of excellence in producing employable graduates by providing support, guidance and counseling.

Mission

To facilitate the career development through professionalism, mannerism, unity and leadership and enabling them to meet employers' expectations by providing a variety of services for enhancing competencies needed by the world of work.

Our Values

We believe in integrity, excellence, inclusivity, and collaboration. Our services are designed to uphold these values and ensure that all students have access to the resources and support they need to succeed.

What We Offer

We offer a wide range of services, including career counseling, resume and cover letter reviews, interview preparation, job search strategies, and networking opportunities. Our team is here to assist students at every step of their career journey.

Our Objectives

The overall objective of the project of which this contract will be a part is as follows:

• To support capacity building of graduates for non-government partners in the field of employment.

• To promote and provide capable graduates in Sri Lanka.

• Serve as a link between university, private sector, and government of Sri Lanka.

• Produce and develop proficient graduates for the Sri Lankan community as well as the international community.

• To assist in providing internationally recognized graduates.

• To support the development of a lifelong career guidance system, which services will be available to SEUSL students, based on their needs in education and occupation selection, in accordance with Sri Lankan employment policy.
